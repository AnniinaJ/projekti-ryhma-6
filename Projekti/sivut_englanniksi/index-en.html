About us

The Alley family has been familiar with animal care for hundreds of generations. Our family business was founded in 1920 and we came to Finland in 1950. Our company is currently run by Richard Alley and Maryam Alley. Our daughter Lane Alley also helps us with animal care.

In addition, our employees include animal caretaker Wilhelmina Grubbly-Plank and sales manager Odero Omolo. We raise, rescue and take care for special and rare animals. These animals are so rare that they are disappearing from the world. Only few people know about their existence, and not many have any treatment experience. We do our best to preserve these wonderful animal species.


Our farm is located in Perä-Savo at Viistokuja 17. We sell animal food and supplies in our online store. We do not have a physical store and we are focused solely on online sales. Larger orders can be picked up from our premises, and home delivery or mailing is also possible.
